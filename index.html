Currently, I am writing my master's thesis at the University of Bamberg. There I study Computing in the Humanities with focus on Human-Computer-Interaction and Information Visualization.

I am particularly interested in the fields of Data-Driven Storytelling and UX/UI Design. For this reason, my master's thesis adresses the question of how to design interactive data representations and the corresponding user interfaces to obtain optimal effectiveness and usability. Hereby, I implement an interactive prototype of one story-telling infographic to compare it with an interactive data comic in the context of a qualitative user study.
